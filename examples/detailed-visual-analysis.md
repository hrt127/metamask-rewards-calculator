**Assumption:** 1 point = 1 LINEA token

**Your allocation:** Points earned = LINEA tokens received

**Pros:**

- Simple, predictable

**Cons:**

- Not confirmed by MetaMask

- Could overshoot if total points are lower

### Scenario B: Proportional Pool Share

**Assumption:** Your share of $30M pool based on % of total points

**Your allocation:** (Your Points / Total Community Points) √ó $30M

**Pros:**

- More likely official interpretation

- Balanced regardless of total participation

**Cons:**

- Can't calculate without knowing total

- Lower payout if many farmers

**Example:**

- Total: 100 billion community points

- You: 100K points (0.0001%)

- Allocation: 0.0001% √ó $30M = **$30** (vs $1,428 at 1:1)
